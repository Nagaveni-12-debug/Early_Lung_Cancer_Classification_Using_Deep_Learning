Common Symptoms of Lung Cancer

Early-stage lung cancer often causes no symptoms. When symptoms appear, they may include:

  • Persistent cough or worsening cough
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Coughing up blood
  • Frequent respiratory infections
  • Fatigue or weakness

If these symptoms persist, medical evaluation is recommended.
