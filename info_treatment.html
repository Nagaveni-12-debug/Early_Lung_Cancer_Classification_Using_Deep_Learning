Treatment Options for Lung Cancer

Treatment depends on the type and stage of cancer. Common treatment options include:

  • Surgery – removal of the tumor in early stages.
  • Radiation Therapy – uses high-energy beams to kill cancer cells.
  • Chemotherapy – drugs that destroy rapidly dividing cells.
  • Immunotherapy – boosts the body's immune system to fight cancer.
  • Targeted Therapy – drugs that block specific cancer cell growth pathways.

Personalized treatment plans often offer the highest success rates.
